The Cubberley Pavilion offers one of the most comfortable, friendly places to dance in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Always wanted to learn? A wide variety of dances (swing, hustle, rumba, tango, waltz, etc.) are taught at beginning, intermediate, and advanced levels on a 'drop-in' basis. (Check our Dance Calendar for the current schedule.)

You never need to bring a partner, and we switch partners frequently so you get a chance to meet and dance with different people. Lively, entertaining dance parties follow each dance lesson.

Then practice what you've learned (or get your dance fix) on the single largest dance floor in the U.S.
